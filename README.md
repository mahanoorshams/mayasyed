## 📂 Featured Projects

### 🏥 NHS RTT Waiting Times Dashboard

Interactive Power BI dashboard tracking NHS Referral to Treatment waiting times across England, January 2021 to March 2025. Custom DAX measures, a rebuilt date table, and synced slicers surface how the waiting list grew from a ~10M pre-Covid baseline to 24M — with Trauma & Orthopaedics consistently the largest share of 52-week breaches.

`Power BI` `DAX` `Power Query` `Healthcare data`

### 🔄 Customer Churn Analysis

Identified high-risk customer segments for a telecoms subscription business using SQL cohort segmentation, Python EDA, and an interactive Tableau dashboard. Key finding: month-to-month fibre customers with no add-on services churned hardest, driving a disproportionate share of revenue lost in the first 90 days.

`SQL` `Python` `Tableau`

[View project →](https://github.com/mayasyed/customer-churn-analysis) · [Live Tableau dashboard →](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/mahanoor.shams/viz/TelcoCustomerChurn_17673780438080/CustomerChurnOverview)

### 💷 EMEA Commercial Revenue Reconciliation

Validated H1 2024 commercial revenue across 7 EMEA markets and 10 media agency partners — cleaning 100 messy records, converting currencies with real month-end FX rates, and classifying variances into Match / Review / Escalate. Outcome: 48 clean matches, 32 reviews, 17 escalations with a structured resolution log.

`Excel` `VLOOKUP` `Variance analysis`
