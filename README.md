### [NHS RTT Waiting Times Dashboard](https://github.com/mayasyed/dbt-ecommerce-portfolio)

Interactive Power BI dashboard tracking NHS Referral to Treatment waiting times across England, January 2021 to March 2025. Custom DAX measures, a rebuilt date table, and synced slicers surface how the waiting list grew from a ~10M pre-Covid baseline to 24M — with Trauma & Orthopaedics consistently the largest share of 52-week breaches.

`Power BI` `DAX` `Power Query` `Healthcare data`
